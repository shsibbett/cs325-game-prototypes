  • Originality: What is original about your game?

  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • The game checks whether the player is overlapping with the 'ice' layer of the tilemap and if they are not colliding with rocks.
    • If both of these conditions are true, then the player slides.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?

    • The easiest part was creating the tilemap and puzzle.
    • The hardest part was figuring out the sliding mechanic; it was difficult to program when the player should slide and when they should be allowed to move again.
