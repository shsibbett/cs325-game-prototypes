Burger Breakout

  • Originality: What is original about your game?
    • The user makes a cheeseburger by breaking the platforms the components are on and catching them on their bun as they fall.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • This game uses game states.
    • The components can be caught in any order and are layered as you catch them. This is done by setting the components to the bun paddle's x coordinate and just slightly above the y coordinate.
    • While each component will always generate on their respective platform, the x value is randomly generated for a different position everytime.
    • This game features levels and resets when all of the burger components have either been caught or have fallen out of bounds.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
    • I learned how to use games states, which was a little difficult to work with at first.
    • I also learned how to set a sprite to move with the mouse pointer and how to set a sprite's value to be randomly generated.
    • The hardest part was getting each burger component to layer properly; I had to figure out what the y value of the component should be if there were already other components of the bun paddle so that the sandwich looked like a sandwich.
    • The 'win a level' conditions were a little hard to nail down. As the player must win a level by catching at least one burger component, there were fifteen different conditions under which a player could 'win' a level as there are four components [2^4 = 16 - 1 (the condition where all of the components fall is considered a 'game over' condition) = 15].

    • The player moves the bun paddle with the mouse.
    • They must use the sesame seed projectile to break the other burger components out of their platforms and catch them as they fall with the bun paddle.
    • The player must also keep the sesame seed in play by hitting it with the bun paddle.
    • 10 points is earned for each brick broken. 100 points are earned for each burger piece caught. 1000 points are earned for completing a level.
    • The player must catch at least one component per level, or it is game over.
    • Once all the burger components have been either caught or lost, everything resets and the next level starts.
    • The player has three lives.
    • If the sesame seed projectile fall to the bottom of the screen, the player loses a life.
    • If the player loses the sesame seed three times, it is game over.
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?
    • To get the highest score, players should try to break as many bricks before collecting or losing all of the burger components as the next level will start as soon as they have done so.
    • Having said that, the player should not prioritize catching a burger piece over catching the sesame seed as lives are more valuable than points and will keep the game going longer.
    • Be careful not to move the bun paddle at the same time as the sesame seed hits it, or the seed's velocity will increase. This could make hitting it more difficult.
