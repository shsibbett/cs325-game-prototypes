No CoordinEggtion

  • Originality: What is original about your game?
    • In this platformer, the player must deal with a control scheme that is randomized every 15 seconds.
    • This forces the player to think on their feet and react quickly to change.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • All the possible controls (WASD and the spacebar) into an array that is randomly selected from without replacement to assign the input mapping.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
    • I learned how to use Tiled to make a tilemap from tilesets.
    • I also learned how to animate a sprite using a spritesheet.
    • The easiest part was randomizing the controls.
    • The hardest part was creating the tilemap and figuring out how to make sure the player collided with the dirt sections of the tileset and not the grass.
    • It was also pretty difficult to make sure that the eggs (which are generated in random locations on the map) were in the bounds of where the player could go.
